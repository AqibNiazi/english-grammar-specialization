# Word Forms and Simple Present Tense

Course 1 of the Learn English: Beginning Grammar Specialization.

## Course Overview

This course focuses on:

- Parts of Speech
- Word Forms
- Subject-Verb Agreement
- Simple Present Tense
- Sentence Construction
